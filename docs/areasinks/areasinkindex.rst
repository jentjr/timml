Area-sinks
----------
There is a circular area-sink, and a strip area-sink. In the future there will be a polygonal area-sink.

1. :class:`~timml.circareasink.CircAreaSink` is used to simulate circular infiltration, or evaporation

2. :class:`~timml.stripareasink.StripAreaSink` is used to simulate infiltration, or evaporation of a strip
